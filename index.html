About Me

My name is Abdul Rashid Moro, an all round photographer based in Accra, Ghana who specializes in fashion, beauty, lifestyle and wedding photography, with passion in perfecting images. I also specialize in Adobe photoshop as well as the use of lightroom in editing images in order to achieve the best. My goal with photography is to capture that feeling of every moment in time -- freezing it to be shared and re-lived over and over again.

I consider photography as my passion more than profession. You can check out some of my works and experiences below.
